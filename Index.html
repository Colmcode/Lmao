Singapore is a relatively young and small nation, yet it is home to many of worldwide known tourist spots for both local and international tourists alike. From Sentosa to Botanical Gardens, Singapore definitely held its own against other well-known countries when it comes to tourism. Most people affectionately know Singapore to be a city in a garden, and as the title has already revealed, the tourist spot of mention today will be Singapore's very own Gardens By The Bay!

What's In Store

Gardens By The Bay is well-known and highly recommended as a tourist spot for many to admire a vast array of plant species both locally and internationally. Gardens By The Bay closely replicates the climates across the world in order to house as many plant species as possible all over the world within its spaces, so as to allow tourists to be able to admire exquisite plant species on display without actually having to travel abroad to various other countires just to view these plant species. It has 2 main conservatories named the Cloud Forest and Flower dome that house over 220000 plant species and has a space area spanning 2.3 hectares or equivalent to 4 football fields worth of beauty. There is also 18 supertrees that has a whooping number of 163000 majestic plant species grown on it! These places mentioned above are not the only places that Gardens By The Bay has in store, for there are many other observatories for sightseeing such as the Floral Fantasy that houses an artistic display of well-arranged flora and fauna and, the newly established Kingfisher Wetlands opened in November 2021 and even food places such as the Conservatory Cafe whereby one can enjoy mouth-watering foods with a view. There are so many more places situated in Gardens By The Bay other than the ones mentioned, and not to forget that there are events for every season and awesome promotions all year round. There is something for everyone at the Gardens By The Bay which certainly makes a tourist spoilt for choices. A memorable time at Gardens By The Bay is highly guarenteed, so why not be a tourist at Gardens By The Bay today!
